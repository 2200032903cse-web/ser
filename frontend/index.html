    About the Project

    Objective: Classify human emotions from speech audio to improve human-computer interaction, mental health monitoring, and customer service.

    Method: Audio is processed with machine learning techniques to detect emotions such as happy, sad, angry, and neutral.

    Output: The system displays the predicted emotion, confidence score, and stores results for later analysis.

    Applications: Mental health monitoring, virtual assistants, and call centers.
